1. Who We Are

Sports Drop Meow Merger ("the Game", "we", "us") is a free browser-based game. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect when you play and how we use it.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the minimum possible data to operate the game:

  • Leaderboard name: If you choose to save your score, you provide a display name (1–20 characters). This is stored publicly on the leaderboard.
  • Score: Your final game score, stored alongside your name if you submit it.
  • Local storage: Your best score is stored locally in your browser. This never leaves your device unless you submit it.

We do not collect: email addresses, real names, passwords, location data, device identifiers, or any personally identifiable information.

3. Google AdSense & Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing history across websites. This is managed by Google, not by us.

Google's advertising cookies include:

  • _ga, _gid: Google Analytics tracking (if enabled)
  • IDE, DSID: Google ad personalisation cookies
  • NID: Used to show personalised ads in Google Search

You can opt out of personalised Google ads at: google.com/settings/ads

You can also manage all cookies via our cookie consent banner when you first visit the game.

4. Ko-fi (Support Button)

We display a "Buy me a coffee" button linking to Ko-fi. If you click it and make a payment, you are subject to Ko-fi's Privacy Policy. We receive only the amount donated — no personal data about you.

5. Database & Score Storage

Leaderboard scores are stored in Supabase (a secure cloud database). Only your chosen display name and score are stored. This data is publicly visible on the leaderboard. Do not use your real name as your display name if you prefer privacy.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase Privacy Policy — database storage
  • Google Privacy Policy — advertising
  • Ko-fi Privacy Policy — donations

7. Data Retention

Leaderboard entries are kept indefinitely unless you contact us to request removal. Local best scores stay in your browser until you clear your browser data.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your leaderboard entry
  • Opt out of personalised advertising (via Google's settings)
  • Clear your local game data (via your browser settings → Clear Site Data)

9. Children's Privacy

This game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. The game does not require account creation or any personal information to play.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, please contact us via Ko-fi or raise an issue on our GitHub repository.
